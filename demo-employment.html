Employment

RightNOW Plumbing is a fast-growing company built upon honest customer service and flat-fee pricing. We believe the key to providing the best customer service is to employ highly skilled workers and support a healthy work/life balance.

 

Are you a Plumbing Professional looking for more than a job?
RightNOW Plumbing wants to speak to you!

Job Duties

  • Perform residential with some commercial plumbing services including diagnosing issue, performing repairs and or installation, and ensure customer satisfaction with little to no supervision.
  • Assess plumbing issues and provide quote based upon flat-fee system.
  • Inspect work sites to determine condition or necessary repairs, review blueprints or specifications to determine work requirements.
  • Assemble pipe sections, tubing, or fittings, using couplings, clamps, screws, bolts, cement, plastic solvents, caulking, or soldering, brazing, or welding equipment.
  • Install pipe assemblies, fittings, valves, appliances such as dishwashers or water heaters, or fixtures such as sinks or toilets, using hand or power tools.
  • Ensure thorough understanding of work order prior to arrival, perform assessment, and coordinate directly with Management for changes and client invoicing coordination.
  • Provide courteous and timely customer service with clean, presentable appearance.
  • Ensure customer thoroughly understands work order content before signing off.
  • Complete and timely submit all work order paperwork and project status to management on a daily basis.
  • Maintain thorough records of work assignments.
  • Ensure adherence to all local, state, and federal standards, code, and regulations.
  • Clean and maintain tools and equipment.
  • Informally direct Helper/Assistants throughout project or portions of project in pipe cutting, pre-assembly, or installation of plumbing systems or components.
  • Work weekends, on-call, and additional hours as needed.

Requirements

  • Experience with providing plumbing services and working directly with residential customers
  • Maintenance of work truck and materials
  • Clean driving record and valid Virginia driver's license
  • Must pass background check
  • Ability to lift 75lbs

Benefits

  • Health, Vision, Dental and Life insurance for full-time employees
  • Gym membership with pool
  • Bonuses based upon efficiency
  • Hourly salary negotiable depending upon experience
